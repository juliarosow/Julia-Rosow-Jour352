Indoor and outdoor heat index during Baltimore heat wave, July 16-22, 2019

A heat wave hit Baltimore in July 2019. Sensors recored the heat index inside a Baltimore resident's home as consistently higher than outside heat index. Outdoor heat index topped out at 111 degrees while indoor heat index reached as high as 113 degrees.
